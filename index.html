National Liberty Museum

This is the museum that promotes of freedom and honors global leaders.
There are many sculptures made of glass.

Directions from nearest airport to museum

Nearest Airport from the National Liberty Museum is Philadelphia International Airport.

  1. From the airport, take the road 12A-B.
  2. Then, take the exit from 12A-B.
    1. Leads to PA-291 toward Cargo City.
    2. Travel on road for 9.4 miles.
  3. Take the ramp on the left onto I-95S.
    1. From here, travel 0.2 miles.
  4. Turn right onto Market St and then turn left onto S 3rd St.
